The Felix Game

HOW TO PLAY

OBJECTIVE: Reach the flag at the end of each level!


CONTROLS:

Move Left/Right: WASD or Arrow Keys

Jump: W or Up Arrow

Double Jump: Hold W or Up Arrow

Fireball: F

Levitation: Space Bar


TIPS:

Use your abilities to shoot enemies

Use power-ups to gain special abilities

Do not jump on enemies to defeat them

Avoid falling into pits!
